Science Olympiad

This was my first year in Science Olympiad, but I decided to join because a friend told me it didn't interfere with robotics for once. I joined Robot Tour and Electric Vehicle (EV) because both events played into my interests of 3d printing, electronics and programming. The goal of EV is to have your robot drive a distance between 7 and 10 meters as quickly and accurately as possible. Robot tour required a robot that was able to be programmed at the event to navigate a maze. V1 of both competed at regionals, and both qualified for state. To prepare for state I redesigned both events. I focused on reducing weight for EV while improving consistency, and I moved to a 4 motor design with room for encoders for Robot Tour. State is on April 5th-6th, so I will update how things went after that.
